Coach

Coaching and developing your team has never been easier or more impactful. iCoachFirst has taken the Field Coaching process to the next level through customizable coaching forms, “between-sessions” feedback tools, unprecedented analysis, and real-time access to corporate learning assets to ensure coaching is an on-going, continuous process.

Train

Ensure coaching and training is an integrated and continuous process to achieve development objectives consistently and comprehensively across your Field Sales. iCoachFirst’s mobile LMS encourages engagement and ongoing collaboration between field trainers, managers and representatives to achieve superior and timely skills development and knowledge dissemination.

Assess

Incorporate measurement tools directly into your coaching, training, and development processes to identify baselines, evaluate skill gaps, and measure learning with iCoachFirst. Strengthen individual and organizational performance management capacity with the Assess module.

Connect

Reach your teams quickly and effectively, and encourage them to share success stories, ideate and collaborate with the iCoachFirst Connect module.

Meetings

Ensure engagement of all attendees in the learning and interaction process before, during and after an actual event to guarantee meaningful experience with iCoachFirst. The Meeting Management module will make sure that follow up and meetings are easy and effective despite dispersed teams, varying venues and complex agendas.

Leadership

An end-to-end solution to identify top performers and develop skills unique to each career path, iCoachFirst ensures that your top performers are engaged, connected and ready for the future.
